Quit India Movement

August 1942 to February 1944

On August 8, 1942, the Quit India Movement ignited, A watershed moment when India's freedom was ignited. Mahatma Gandhi, with his words so profound, Urged the nation to rise and shake off the colonial ground. Mass protests swept across the land, challenging British rule, Met with brutal repression, but the flame of freedom fueled. Thousands were arrested, and countless lives were lost, Yet, the spirit of independence stood firm at any cost. The Quit India Movement marked a turning tide, As India's resolve to be free could no longer be denied.
